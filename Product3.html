NVIDIA's Ultimate Gaming GPU

The Nvidia GeForce RTX 4090 is a flagship GPU built on Ada Lovelace architecture, offering groundbreaking performance for gaming, AI, and content creation. It features 16,384 CUDA cores, 24 GB of GDDR6X VRAM, and advanced ray tracing with DLSS 3 support for ultra-realistic graphics. Designed for 4K gaming and demanding workloads, it excels in efficiency and AI-driven enhancements. The card’s high power draw (450W TDP) requires robust cooling but delivers unrivaled speed and visuals in modern applications.

Key Specifications

  • Architecture: Ada Lovlace
  • CUDA Cores: 16,384
  • Boost Clock: 2.52GHz to 2.55GHz
  • Memory: 24 GB GDDR6X
  • Memory Speed: 21 Gbps
  • Power Consumption: 450W
  • Recommended PSU: 850W
